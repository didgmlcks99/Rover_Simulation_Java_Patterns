Rover Running

Each Wheel Turns Adapting to Terrain State

  • Turning Front Left wheel
  • Slipper Ground --> Extrude Spike from Front Left Wheel Shoe
  • Turning Front Right wheel
  • Slipper Ground --> Extrude Spike from Front Right Wheel Shoe
  • Turning Back Left wheel
  • Mushy Ground --> Widen Back Left Wheel Width
  • Turning Back Right wheel
  • Mushy Ground --> Widen Back Right Wheel Width
