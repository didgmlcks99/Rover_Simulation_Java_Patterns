Rover Running

Each Wheel Turns Adapting to Terrain State

  • Turning Front Left wheel
  • Mushy Ground --> Widen Front Left Wheel Width
  • Turning Front Right wheel
  • Mushy Ground --> Widen Front Right Wheel Width
  • Turning Back Left wheel
  • Slipper Ground --> Extrude Spike from Back Left Wheel Shoe
  • Turning Back Right wheel
  • Mushy Ground --> Widen Back Right Wheel Width

Each Motor Controls Power Consumption

  • Control of Front Left Motor
  • RMP Decreased --> Increase Power to Front Left Motor
  • Control of Front Right Motor
  • RMP Increased --> Decrease Power to Front Right Motor
  • Control of Back Left Motor
  • Constant RMP --> Keep the Same Power to Back Left Motor
  • Control of Back Right Motor
  • Constant RMP --> Keep the Same Power to Back Right Motor
